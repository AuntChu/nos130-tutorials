THE LESSON IN THE COUNT

Four differing lines, but only THREE faults: the DNS change and the failed internet test are one fault and its downstream symptom. Anomalies are leads, not verdicts; two leads can share one cause. Counting your diff lines as faults is how techs end up fixing the same problem twice.
