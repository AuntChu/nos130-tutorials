The Network Detective

Master the four numbers, the toolbox, and the troubleshooting ladder. Then solve four real case files in a simulated terminal.

01Briefing

Half of every help desk queue is one sentence: I cannot reach something. This tutorial trains the method that turns that sentence into a five minute diagnosis. Everything runs offline in this page, including a working simulated terminal.

YOU WILL LEARN

Read any ipconfig output like a sentence. Run the four toolbox commands. Climb the five-rung ladder in order. Map symptoms to the exact rung that catches them.

HOW SCORING WORKS

Case Files: 8 points (4 cases, rung + cause each). Knowledge Check: 8 points. 16 total. Your running score lives in the bar at the bottom. 13 or better is detective grade.

TERMINAL TIPS

Every terminal here accepts real commands: ipconfig, ipconfig /all, ipconfig /flushdns, ping <target>, nslookup <name>, tnc <name> (short for Test-NetConnection), cls, and help. Type them exactly as you would on a real machine.
